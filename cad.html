Computer Aided Design


How-to resize:

  1. 1.Install & open Gimp
  2. 2.Open Image->Hover over Image tab->Click Scale Image
  3. 3.Choose the measurement for your Image->Click Scale once done

How-to remove & replace Background:

  1. 1.Continuing from above, Click on Foreground Select Tool->Trace the object/person->Hit enter
  2. 2.Highlight the object/person, making sure it's no longer covered in any colours->click enter
  3. 3.Go to the paths tab->Click Selection to path
  4. 4.Right click the original layer->Add Layer Mask->Choose selection->Click add
  5. 5.Open the backrgound you would like->Resize->Copy & Paste into the layers tab
  6. 6.Convert the floating layer to an actual layer
  7. 7.Enjoy :)
